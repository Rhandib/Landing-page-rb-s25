About Synth & Stitch

We are an AI authentic vintage clothing brand curated by code, reimagining fashion with a focus on sustainability and unique design.

  • Sustainability Angle: We use AI to generate upcycled and sustainable fashion plans, preventing waste by transforming old garments into new, stylish pieces.
  • Vibe Track Playlists: Each piece comes with an AI-generated "vibe track" playlist, perfectly matched to the clothing's decade and style.
